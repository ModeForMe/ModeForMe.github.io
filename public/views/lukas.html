Lukas Gschwandtner

Bags are my absolute passion. At the age of 12 years I was seized with the desire and the love of craftsmanship. For hours I sat with excitement at my workplace to implement my ideas and my creativity in handbags. From the early days was passion that brought them together with my brother and my family great support establishing my own label. To broaden my horizons I visited the Vienna Fashion School in Hetzendorf. I was allowed to sell my bags in Dantendorfer Store in Salzburg with much success. Purism, simplicity and fine detail characterise my style. The unique are made ​​of quality leather and recycled, purified furs.
